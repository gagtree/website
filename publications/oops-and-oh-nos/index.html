Oops & Oh No's: Scored Journal of All the Funny and Dumb Things We Can't Unsay or Undo

January 2025

Tired of forgetting the absolute gems of hilarious nonsense your friends, coworkers, or family blurt out? Look no further! This logbook is here to immortalize those epic moments of verbal chaos.

This laugh-out-loud journal is your ultimate tool for documenting every face-melting quote, accidental joke, and brain-fart blunder. With its fun scoring system, you’ll rank the best (or worst!) episodes and create a leaderboard of unforgettable moments. It’s also a great tool for team building and bonding, since we can be sure that all humans will human eventually.

  • Easy-to-Use Layout: Includes space for the when, who, reporter, context, episode, and a fun review and scoring board on each page
  • Perfect for Sharing: Bring it to company review meetings, team parties, project workshops, or the breakroom for endless fun
  • An Amazing Gift: Ideal for your boss, coworkers, teammates, friends, and family

Don’t let hilarious moments disappear into thin air - turn them into memories you’ll never stop laughing about! Start building your score today!
